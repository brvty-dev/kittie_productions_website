Workplace Policies

Kittie Productions is committed to acting ethically and with integrity in all business dealings and relationships, and to implement effective systems to enable it to comply with the following policies.

Code of Conduct

Kittie Productions is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional working environment.

All cast, crew, and collaborators are expected to:

  • Treat others with respect and professionalism
  • Refrain from harassment, bullying, or discriminatory behaviour
  • Follow direction and production protocols
  • Respect boundaries, particularly during sensitive scenes

Any breach of this code may result in removal from the production.

Freelancers

Freelance creatives and contractors are hired on a per project basis.

We are committed to:

  • Clear agreements and expectations
  • Fair and timely payment
  • Proper crediting of work

Freelancers are expected to:

  • Deliver work to agreed standards
  • Respect confidentiality
  • Comply with company policies

We aim to build long-term, respectful working relationships.

Working Hours and Welfare

Kittie Productions is committed to maintaining reasonable working conditions.

We aim to:

  • Avoid excessive working hours
  • Provide appropriate breaks
  • Monitor workload during productions

We recognise the importance of wellbeing in maintaining a safe and productive environment.

Data Security

Kittie Productions handles sensitive creative and personal data, including scripts, production materials, and user information.

We are committed to:

  • Protecting confidential information from unauthorised access
  • Restricting access based on user permissions
  • Using secure systems to store and transfer data

All users and collaborators must:

  • Respect confidentiality of materials
  • Not share or distribute content without permission
  • Report any suspected data breaches

Breaches may result in removal of access and legal action where appropriate.

Digital Ethics

Kittie Productions recognises the growing role of artificial intelligence (AI) as a tool in the creative industries.

If it is used when creating content, we are committed to:

  • Using AI responsibly and transparently
  • Respecting intellectual property rights
  • Avoiding misleading or deceptive content

Kittie Productions will not use AI to replicate individuals without consent or to create deceptive media.

Intellectual Property

All intellectual property created as part of Kittie Productions projects remains the property of Kittie Productions unless otherwise agreed in writing.

This includes:

  • Scripts
  • Visual materials
  • Designs
  • Production assets

Contributors will be credited appropriately in accordance with agreed terms.

Unauthorised use, reproduction, or distribution of materials is prohibited.

Equal Opportunities

Kittie Productions is committed to providing equal opportunities in employment and production.

We do not discriminate on the basis of:

  • Age
  • Disability
  • Gender reassignment
  • Marriage or civil partnership
  • Pregnancy or maternity
  • Race
  • Religion or belief
  • Sex
  • Sexual orientation

We aim to ensure fair treatment in:

  • Recruitment
  • Training
  • Promotion
  • Working conditions

All decisions are based on merit and business needs.

Diversity & Inclusion

Kittie Productions values diversity and is committed to creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels respected and able to contribute.

We aim to:

  • Encourage diverse perspectives in our productions
  • Work with talent from a wide range of backgrounds
  • Create a respectful and collaborative working culture

We recognise that diversity strengthens creativity and storytelling.

Discrimination, harassment, or exclusionary behaviour will not be tolerated.

Safeguarding

Kittie Productions is committed to safeguarding the welfare of children and vulnerable adults involved in our productions.

We will:

  • Provide a safe working environment
  • Ensure appropriate supervision is in place
  • Conduct relevant background checks where required
  • Follow legal and regulatory guidance

All staff, contractors and freelancers have a responsibility to:

  • Report concerns immediately
  • Act in the best interests of those at risk

Safeguarding concerns will be treated seriously and handled in accordance with best practice.

Children and Young People

Kittie Productions recognises that children and young people require additional protection and care.

Where our productions involve individuals under the age of 18, we are committed to ensuring their safety, wellbeing, and dignity at all times.

We will:

  • Ensure appropriate supervision is in place at all times
  • Work with licensed chaperones where required by law
  • Obtain necessary permissions from parents or guardians
  • Conduct risk assessments specific to child involvement
  • Ensure working hours comply with legal requirements
  • Provide a safe and respectful working environment

All staff, contractors and freelancers are expected to:

  • Maintain appropriate professional boundaries
  • Avoid any behaviour that could be perceived as inappropriate
  • Report any concerns immediately

Any safeguarding concerns involving a child will be treated as a priority and handled in accordance with relevant legal and safeguarding guidance.

Health & Safety

Kittie Productions is committed to ensuring the health, safety, and wellbeing of all employees, contractors, and participants.

We aim to:

  • Provide safe working conditions
  • Conduct risk assessments for all productions
  • Ensure equipment is used safely
  • Promote a culture of safety awareness

All staff, contractors and freelancers are expected to:

  • Follow safety procedures
  • Report hazards or incidents
  • Take reasonable care of themselves and others

Health and safety is a shared responsibility across all productions.
